# Your Co-Pastor — Landing Page Showcase

Internal design directory for reviewing landing page options before launch.

## How to Use

1. Open `index.html` in your browser
2. Browse the 3 design directions  
3. Click any card to preview the full landing page
4. Share feedback — pages will be updated based on your input

## 2026 Landing Page Checklist

All pages comply with:
- Hero: 1 promise, 1 audience, 1 CTA, trust badge above fold
- Copy: Headline under 10 words, benefit-driven, customer language
- Structure: Hero → Pain → Solution → Proof → CTA
- Social proof: Specific testimonials with results + metrics
- Technical: No localStorage, mobile-first, single scroll flow
